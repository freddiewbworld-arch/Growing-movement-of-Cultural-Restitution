# Growing-movement-of-Cultural-Restitution
This is a repository for ap roject that seeks to explore the growing movement of Cultural Restitution in the United Kingdom. British institutions outside of the British Museum are starting to return artefacts they held in their posession that had been acquired during the colonial era. Now that we are no longer in this era and we are able to reflect on the actions we have taken to acquire artefacts. The movement for cultural restitution, returning artefacts back to their owners, is starting to make an effort to leave behind the colonial past of this country. Leaving behind does not mean forgetting what has been done, but returning objects to their original owners and seeking to educate the public on why they should be returned.

This repository contains the code used to scrape the articles gathered for this analysis. You can find these under the .ipynb files that are labelled with what they do in the file name. Inside the dataset folder you will be able to find the original articles that were scraped from the website, and also find all the articles combined into one. Including documentation on the cleaning the data has been through.
